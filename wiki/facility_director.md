## Overview

The **Facility Director**, commonly shortened to **FD**, is an executive facility team that commands the entire facility.  
They are the **highest rank** in the facility, above Council Executives.

Facility Directors are able to:
- Access any team locker
- Open doors without requiring a keycard
- Broadcast announcements without being in the Broadcast Room
- Control major facility systems using the **Facility Control Tablet**

## Gameplay

The Facility Director spawns in the **Strategic Command Center (SCC)** inside the Directorâ€™s Office.

They have near-total access to the facility, excluding admin-only areas.  
Due to their powerful weapon access, Facility Directors often act as the primary defenders against raids.

Additional abilities include:
- Facility-wide announcements
- Alert level control
- Access to the **Master Teleporter Room**

Purchasing the gamepass also allows players to:
- Obtain Director items from the armory without being on the FD team
- Access all executive vehicles
